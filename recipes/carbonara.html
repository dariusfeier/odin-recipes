Gluten Free Carbonara

Description

This gluten-free carbonara is a simple, time-efficient, and delicious dish that is guaranteed to make your mouth water. Perfect for those who choose to omit gluten from their diets or live with celiac disease.

With a total time of 35 minutes, you will be enjoying a classic Italian pasta dish with a healthier twist while also having time for your upcoming halloween festivities

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) box of Barilla Gluten Free Spaghetti
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup chopped raw bacon
  • 1 bundle of asparagus, trimmed, cut 1 inch long ob a bias b
  • 5 large egg yolks, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to your liking
