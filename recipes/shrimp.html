Creamy Chipotle Shrimp

Description

This is creamy chipotle shrimp recipe is a mouth-watering dish that can be whipped up in just 30 minutes

This meal is bound to catch the eyes of your guests. More importantly, its creamy, zesty, and slight kick will blow their minds and taste buds.

total time: 30 minutes

Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil, dividedi
  • 1 pound shrimp
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 green onions, slcied with green and white parts separated
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 chipotle sauce recipe: chipotle sauce
  • 1/2 cup milk, dairy or non-dairy
  • garnish with chipped cilanto and sliced jalapeño

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a saute pan on medium heat. Add the shrimp and saute for 3-4 minutes, or until the shrimp are cooked through. Move them to a bowl once finished
  2. In same pan, add another tablespoon of olive oil and add the bell pepper and saute for 4-5 minutes
  3. Next add the garlic, white parts of the green onion, salt and pepper. Stir for another 2 minutes
  4. Pour in chipotle sauce and milk. Stir to combine. Then add shrimp back to the pan to warm up
  5. Garnish with green parts of green onion, chopped cilantro and sliced jalapeño pepper.
